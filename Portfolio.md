# 📂 Portfolio Narrative

Welcome to my portfolio of projects. My work bridges the gap between advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) and blockchain technologies, focusing on distributed systems, autonomous agents, and real-time data processing.

Here is an overview of my primary projects and how they relate to one another:

## 🤖 AI Projects

### [AgentSystem](./README.md)
**Distributed AI orchestration system optimized for edge computing.**
AgentSystem is the foundational framework for building and orchestrating AI workloads across multiple devices, including resource-constrained edge devices like the Raspberry Pi 5. It provides a distributed task queue, multi-provider AI support (OpenAI, Gemini, xAI, Local), and autonomous operations with memory persistence.

### EBAIF (Enterprise Blockchain AI Framework)
**Core architecture and concepts for intelligent agents.**
EBAIF serves as the conceptual sandbox for advanced AI behavior and enterprise integration. Many of the autonomous behaviors, memory systems, and architectural patterns developed in EBAIF are actively used and refined across my other projects.

## 🔗 Blockchain Projects

### AgentSwarm
**Real-time blockchain monitoring with AI-driven whale tracking on Solana.**
AgentSwarm applies the autonomous agent concepts pioneered in EBAIF to the fast-paced world of cryptocurrency. By leveraging AI, it monitors on-chain data in real-time, tracking major liquidity movements ("whales") and providing actionable intelligence. *AgentSwarm uses concepts from EBAIF, optimized for blockchain.*

### Sol_Horse
**Solana blockchain utility and analysis.**
A complementary project to AgentSwarm, Sol_Horse focuses on deep integration with the Solana ecosystem, providing the essential infrastructure and smart contract interactions needed to build robust blockchain applications.

*(Note: AgentSwarm and Sol_Horse share common blockchain infrastructure and may be consolidated into a single monorepo for easier cross-project reuse.)*

## 🌟 The Vision
My ultimate goal is to build intelligent, autonomous systems that can seamlessly operate across both traditional compute environments (like cloud and edge networks via **AgentSystem**) and decentralized networks (like Solana via **AgentSwarm**).

By separating the core AI frameworks from the domain-specific blockchain implementations, I ensure that each project remains focused, maintainable, and highly reusable.
